Our Services

Citizen AI provides a comprehensive suite of services designed to enhance the relationship between citizens and government agencies. Our platform leverages cutting-edge AI technology to deliver efficient, accurate, and accessible services to all citizens.

Government Information Services

Tax Information

Get instant answers to your tax-related questions, including filing procedures, deadlines, deductions, and credits. Our AI assistant can guide you through complex tax scenarios and direct you to relevant resources.

Public Transportation

Access real-time updates on public transportation schedules, route changes, service disruptions, and fare information. Plan your journeys efficiently with our integrated transit information system.

Permit Applications

Learn about permit requirements, application processes, and necessary documentation for various civic activities. Get guidance on business licenses, construction permits, event approvals, and more.

Civic Engagement Services

Policy Information

Stay informed about current policies, proposed legislation, and government initiatives. Understand how new policies might affect you and your community.

Public Services Directory

Find contact information, locations, and hours of operation for various government offices and public services in your area.

Educational Resources

Access educational materials about civic responsibilities, voting procedures, and how government works. Enhance your understanding of democratic processes.

Advanced AI Features

Sentiment Analysis

Our platform analyzes citizen feedback to identify trends in public opinion, helping government agencies understand community needs and concerns.

Issue Tracking

Report civic issues such as potholes, streetlight outages, or park maintenance needs. Track the status of your reports and see how they're being addressed.

Data Insights Dashboard

Government officials can access real-time dashboards showing citizen sentiment, common issues, and service request patterns to make data-driven decisions.
